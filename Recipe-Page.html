Dosa Recipe

About the Dish

Dosa is a traditional South Indian dish made from fermented rice and urad dal (black gram). It’s crispy, light, and usually served with chutney and sambar. It is one of the most loved breakfast items across India.

Dish Image

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Preparation Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4–6 hours.
  2. Grind them together to make a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  3. Allow the batter to ferment overnight or for 8–10 hours.
  4. Add salt and mix well before making dosas.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan or tawa and pour a ladle of batter in the center.
  6. Spread it thin in a circular motion and drizzle some oil around the edges.
  7. Cook until golden brown, then fold and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

Serve dosa with coconut chutney, tomato chutney, and hot sambar for a complete South Indian meal.
